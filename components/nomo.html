Marketplaces need to be compliant by 2018. We can help

In January 2018 the Payments Services Directive 2 (PSD2) changed the way that marketplaces are viewed by the FCA. Businesses that process third party payments are handling client funds, therefore PSD2 requires them to be regulated as a financial institution in order to protect their sellers.
